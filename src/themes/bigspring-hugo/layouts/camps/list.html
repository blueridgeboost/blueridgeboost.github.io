Blue Ridge Boosts offers instructional programs in robotics, coding, and math during the school breaks and days off school. We also offer camps on days with unexpected school closings such as "snow days".
Blue Ridge Boost is a single focus instructional program, not a day care program. As such, students must be at least six years old for camps.
All camps are held at Blue Ridge Boost, 2171 Ivy Rd, Charlottesville.

Half-day students should bring a snack. Full-day students should bring two snacks and lunch.

Join us for an exhilarating exploration of robotics, coding, chess, and math. Teamwork and collaboration are at the heart of our camp experience. Students will have the opportunity to work together, share ideas, and solve challenges. Through hands-on activities and group projects, students will develop critical thinking skills and learn the importance of effective communication.

Typical camp day:

8:30–10:30am Free play, getting to know each other
10:30–11am Snack
11am–12:30pm Guided learning session
12:30–1pm Lunch
1–2:30pm Guided learning session
2:30–3pm Snack
3–5pm Individual and group exploration
