At Blue Ridge Boost, we are dedicated to empowering the next generation of thinkers and innovators. Our classes in math, coding, and robotics are designed to inspire curiosity, foster creativity, and build life-long critical skills.

Monthly subscription based classes run for the entire academic year and can be joined any time. Your card will be charged on the first day of class or current date if the class is in progress and monthly thereafter. Please note:

  • Subscriptions can be canceled at any time.
  • Subscriptions can be rescheduled or transferred to another class.
  • No partial refunds are given for the remainder of the month.
  • Make ups can be scheduled on teacher work days and other times based on instructor availability.

Session-based classes have a predetermined number of meetings.
