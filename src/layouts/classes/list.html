Session-based classes follow a set schedule with a fixed number of meetings, usually between 4 and 8.

Continuous Enrollment Classes

Continuous Enrollment Classes run from September - May and June - August. Payment is due each month on the anniversary of your enrollment.

  • In-Progress classes are currently running and can be joined at any time.
  • Starting Soon classes have scheduled start dates in the near future.
  • On-Demand classes currently have no sign-ups, but we’re excited to start them. If you bring a friend, you’ll both receive a 10% discount on the class.
  • Closed Registration classes have either reached the maximum number of students or are no longer open for enrollment.

Subscription Discount

Choosing a subscription plan not only adds convenience but also saves you 5% on monthly payments. After being a subscription member for three months, you'll receive a 10% discount on our session-based classes, camps, and events!
