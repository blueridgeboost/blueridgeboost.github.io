Your Instructor: Nora Evans

Nora Evans, your instructor, earned Master degrees in Mathematics, Computer Science, and Teaching from the University of Virginia. Nora is passionate about math and computing education, especially in attracting and retaining girls and women in STEM fields. The classroom environment and courses are designed to appeal to all students.

Math Experience

  • in-person tutoring for AP Calculus for over a year
  • teaching internship at Monticello High School in Algebra 2, Precalculus, and Geometry
  • co-founded and organized a math circle for preschool and elementary school age girls
  • teaching assisstant for graduate-level courses at the University of Virginia
  • Financial Mathematics instructor at the University of Virginia

Nora’s experience in computer science education includes:

  • First Lego League team lead and coach 2018-2019 and 2019-2020 seasons

  • several year of industry experience
  • co-author of several research articles
