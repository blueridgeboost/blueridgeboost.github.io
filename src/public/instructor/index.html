Your Instructor: Ana Nora Evans

Nora is passionate about math and computing education, especially in attracting and retaining girls and women in STEM fields. The classroom environment and courses are designed to appeal to all students, not only cameo’s and boys.

Ana Nora Evans graduated from Polytechnical University of Bucharest, Romania with a degree in Computer Sience. She earned Masters degrees in Mathematics, Computer Science from the University of Virginia. She will earn a Master of Teaching degree in secondary math education from the University of Virginia in May 2022.

Nora co-founded and organized a math circle for girls ages 3 to 9 for four years. She also has four years of experience tutoring elementary students in math and coding.

Nora has classroom experience as a student-teacher at Monticello High School with Algebra 2, Math Analysis, Geometry, and Computer Science. She also has one year experience in both online and in-person tutoring of high school students in math and computer science.

Nora taught one section of Financial Mathematics at the University of Virginia and has been a teching assistant for graduate level algebra. In Computer Science, Nora was a teaching assistant for several college classes including Java programming, operating systems, distributed systems, compilers, algorithms, and programming languages. She also has one year of experience in online tutoring college students in math and computer science, both in-person and online.

Nora is an experienced software engineer with industry and research experience. She is a co-author of several research papers published in top conferences in computer security and software engineering.

About Blue Ridge Boost

Enrichment and tutoring for ambitious achievers, led by Ana Nora Evans in Charlottesville, Virginia. We provide instruction in Math and Computer Science to any student interested in learning.
