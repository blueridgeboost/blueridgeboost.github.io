1-Day Camps

Hands-on STEM programs during school breaks, snow days, and teacher workdays for ages 6–13. Half-day (8:30 AM–1:00 PM) and full-day (8:30 AM–5:00 PM) options — all materials included.

Topics

Robotics — build and program robots
Coding & Game Design — Minecraft Education and Roblox Studio
Strategic Games — board games and logic puzzles

How It Works

Learning sessions: Topic-related activities
Exploration sessions: Try a different topic or free play (with parent approval)
Breaks: Screen-free snack and lunch — board games encouraged

Logistics

Location: 2171 Ivy Rd, Charlottesville
Ages: 6–13 (must be at least 6)
Bring: Snack (half-day) · 2 snacks + lunch (full-day)
