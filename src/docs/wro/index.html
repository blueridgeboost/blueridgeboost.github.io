WRO-USEL Regional Open Qualifier 2026

Blue Ridge Boost is an official host of a World Robot Olympiad-USEL Regional Open Qualifier for the 2026 season. Teams compete right here in Charlottesville, Virginia, for a chance to represent the USA at international Open Championships.

Qualify for International Open Championships 2026

🌎 Americas Open Championship 2026

  • Categories: All WRO competition categories
  • Age groups: All age groups
  • Spots: 1 per age group / category

Top-placing teams qualify directly for the Americas Open Championship.

🌍 Non-Americas Open Championships 2026

  • Regions: Europe, Asia, Middle East and North Africa
  • Categories: TBD
  • Spots: Minimum 1 (additional spots allocated once confirmed by USEL)

Spots are awarded to top placement teams (1st, 2nd, 3rd) in their respective categories.

Event Details

Event: USEL Regional Open Qualifier
Season: 2026 WRO USA
Location: Charlottesville, VA
Dates: Coming soon
Organized by: Blue Ridge Boost
Sanctioned by: USEL Youth Robotics

Competition Categories

🤖 RoboMission

Program autonomous robots to complete missions on a themed playing field. Available at Elementary, Junior, and Senior levels.

⚽ RoboSports

Build robots that compete head-to-head in sports-themed challenges. Teamwork, strategy, and engineering combined.

💡 Future Innovators

Design a robotics-based solution to a real-world problem. Combine creativity, research, and engineering in an open-ended project.

🏎️ Future Engineers

Build a self-driving vehicle that navigates a track autonomously. A challenging category focused on sensors, AI, and precision control.

Team Eligibility

To participate, teams must:

  • Hold a valid USEL Affiliate Season Pass — or register directly through Blue Ridge Boost for this event (no separate pass required)
  • Meet WRO age and category eligibility requirements
  • Teams of 2–3 members plus a coach

Why Compete at Blue Ridge Boost?

🎯 Direct Path to Championships

Top teams qualify for the Americas Open Championship and international events in Europe, Asia, and MENA regions.

👥 Community-Centered

A welcoming, well-organized competition run by experienced STEM educators who care about every team's experience.

📍 Local & Accessible

Compete right here in Charlottesville, VA — no long-distance travel required for teams in the Mid-Atlantic region.
