  Level 3–4: Week-by-Week Plan (MOEMS and Math Kangaroo) 

   
   Week 1 
   Theme: Introduction (Foundations and mixed skills) 
   Focus skills: Counting, basic combinatorics, digit properties, simple set counts, multi-step problems 
   Strategy spotlight: Careful reading; simple sketches/tallies 
   Practice test: Intro mixed mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 2 
   MOEMS Focus: Reinforce Introduction skills using MOEMS-style problems 
   Emphasis: Precise parsing, smart estimation, simple case splits 
   Practice test: MOEMS mini-set on foundational skills (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 3 
   Theme: Patterns 
   Focus skills: Numeric/symbolic patterns, row-sum balance, cyclic seating, cube color adjacency 
   Strategy spotlight: Find a Pattern; verify with small cases 
   Practice test: Patterns mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 4 
   MOEMS Focus: Pattern problems aligned to Week 3 
   Emphasis: Recurrence spotting, modular repeats, pattern extension 
   Practice test: MOEMS patterns mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 5 
   Theme: Draw a Picture 
   Focus skills: Area/counting by parts, queue modeling, maze paths, growth comparisons, flips/reflections 
   Strategy spotlight: Draw a Picture; annotate paths/counts 
   Practice test: Diagram-based set (start sketches in class, complete at home) 
   

   
   Week 6 
   MOEMS Focus: Diagramming and visual reasoning 
   Emphasis: Translating text → picture, marking invariants on diagrams 
   Practice test: MOEMS visual mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 7 
   Theme: Make a List 
   Focus skills: Exhaustive casework, digit-sum constructions, maximizing sums, ordered selections, permutations, points feasibility 
   Strategy spotlight: Organized List; group by cases 
   Practice test: Listing/permutations set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 8 
   MOEMS Focus: Systematic listing and casework 
   Emphasis: Case partitioning, avoiding double counting 
   Practice test: MOEMS casework mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 9 
   Theme: Guess and Check 
   Focus skills: Maximization by pruning digits, digit-sum constraints, grid scanning, multi-clue name logic, size parity pairing 
   Strategy spotlight: Guess–Check–Revise; track attempts 
   Practice test: Guess-and-check mini-set (attempt in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 10 
   MOEMS Focus: Efficient guessing with constraints 
   Emphasis: Bounding, parity checks, targeted trials 
   Practice test: MOEMS guess-and-check mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 11 
   Theme: Make a Table 
   Focus skills: Temperature sequences, operator outcomes, calendar feasibility, linear combinations, coin reachability 
   Strategy spotlight: Build a Table; pattern spotting 
   Practice test: Table-building tasks (set up in class, complete at home) 
   

   
   Week 12 
   MOEMS Focus: Data tables and pattern extraction 
   Emphasis: Row/column labeling, incremental fills, spotting regularity 
   Practice test: MOEMS table mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 13 
   Theme: Guess and Check 
   Focus skills: Structured trials with provided figures, constraint satisfaction using visuals 
   Strategy spotlight: Use handout figures to document/prune 
   Practice test: Visual guess-and-check set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 14 
   MOEMS Focus: Visual guess/check extensions 
   Emphasis: Recording trials, eliminating families of cases 
   Practice test: MOEMS visual constraints mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 15 
   Theme: Act it Out 
   Focus skills: Physical/constructive reasoning with cutouts, nets to dice, domino tiling, cup moves, square recombination 
   Strategy spotlight: Act it Out; manipulate pieces 
   Practice test: Hands-on mini-set (begin builds in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 16 
   MOEMS Focus: Acting out combinatorial processes 
   Emphasis: Invariants during moves, constructive counterexamples 
   Practice test: MOEMS manipulation mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 17 
   Theme: Work Backwards (Handout) 
   Focus skills: Reverse multi-step processes; calendar/time reversal 
   Strategy spotlight: Work Backwards; reverse steps explicitly 
   Practice test: Backward-reasoning set (start in class, complete at home) 
   

   
   Week 18 
   MOEMS Focus: Reverse reasoning drills 
   Emphasis: Undoing operations, checking by forward replay 
   Practice test: MOEMS backwards mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 19 
   Theme: Logical Reasoning (Handout) 
   Focus skills: Multi-clue deduction; grid/placement logic; elimination strategies 
   Strategy spotlight: Organize clues in a simple grid 
   Practice test: Logic puzzle set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 20 
   MOEMS Focus: Logic grid and elimination problems 
   Emphasis: Contradiction spotting, must/cannot placements 
   Practice test: MOEMS logic mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 21 
   Theme: Time and Calendar (Homework set) 
   Focus skills: Travel timelines; day offsets; burner scheduling; custom calendars; rate-time differences 
   Strategy spotlight: Clocks/calendars; timeline bars 
   Practice test: Time/calendar mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 22 
   MOEMS Focus: Scheduling and time arithmetic 
   Emphasis: Overlaps, parallel tasks, modular weekdays 
   Practice test: MOEMS time/calendar mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 23 
   Theme: Geometry (Homework set) 
   Focus skills: Counting rectangles; complementary cuts; right-side views; perimeter scaling; painted surfaces 
   Strategy spotlight: Visualize 2D/3D; systematic counts 
   Practice test: Geometry mini-set (start in class, finish at home) 
   

   
   Week 24 
   MOEMS Focus: Geometry counting and nets 
   Emphasis: Decomposition, symmetry, layer/face accounting 
   Practice test: MOEMS geometry mini-set (start in class, finish at home)
