One-Day Camps

Instructional programs during school breaks and days off.

Choose Your Topic:

  • Robotics
  • Coding with Minecraft Education
  • Game design with Roblox Studio
  • Strategic games

Program Options:

  • Half-day program: two learning sessions + one exploration session
  • Full-day program: four learning sessions + two exploration sessions

Learning sessions: Campers participate in topic-related activities.
Exploration sessions: Campers choose from continuing with the chosen topics, explore a different topic, or play Minecraft or Roblox (with parent approval).
Break times: Screens are not allowed during snack and lunch breaks. Campers are encouraged to play board games.

Camp Details

  • Location: Blue Ridge Boost, 2171 Ivy Road, Charlottesville, Virginia 22903
  • Ages: 6 to 13 years old (all students must be at least 6 to attend)
  • Half-day students: Please bring a snack
  • Full-day students: Please bring two snacks and lunch
  • Drop-off: Starts at 8:30 AM
  • Pick-up: 1:00 PM for half-day, 5:00 PM for full-day

Early Bird Discount

Register early for our 1-day camps and save 25% on your registration fee! This offer is available until three days before the camp.

Additional Information
