Computer Coding at Blue Ridge Boost

Small group classes

We are dedicated to empowering the next generation of thinkers and innovators. Our classes in math, coding, and robotics are designed to inspire curiosity, foster creativity, and build life-long critical skills. Enjoy our monthly subscription classes that run from September through May - you can join at any time during the season. After being a subscription member for three months, you'll receive a 10% discount on our session-based classes, camps, and special events. Our session-based classes have a set schedule with a fixed number of meetings, making it easy to plan ahead.

These classes are for ages 6 to 18. Adults classes are available here.
