  • Experienced, background-checked instructors with active supervision at all times.

FAQs

Do beginners fit in?

Yes! We onboard new players and pair them with supportive instructors and peers.

Can siblings stay together?

We do our best to seat siblings or friends together upon request.

Do kids need their own accounts?

No. We can use guest or program accounts when needed.

Is voice chat allowed?

Only in supervised, private channels when appropriate.

What should they bring?

Water bottle; optional headset. All equipment provided.
