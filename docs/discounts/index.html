Loyalty discounts

At Blue Ridge Boost, we understand the importance of nurturing your child's thirst for knowledge and their eagerness to learn. That's why we're excited to introduce two incredible discount codes that can be applied to all our classes, providing your children with even more opportunities to explore and grow.

Discount Code: CURIOSITY50

If your child is eager to enroll in multiple classes within the same session, we have a fantastic offer for you. Simply pay the full price for the first class, and for any additional classes your student takes during the same session, apply the discount code "CURIOSITY50" at checkout. This code will grant you a remarkable 50% off the regular price of those additional classes, allowing your child to delve into a range of captivating subjects and expand their horizons.

Please note that the "CURIOSITY50" discount code applies only to classes taken by the same student during the same session. It does not apply to siblings or multiple students.

Discount Code: SIBLINGS10

We also recognize the value of education for siblings and believe in making it more accessible to families. With our "SIBLINGS10" discount code, paying for the first child's class in full unlocks a special offer for subsequent children. Use the code at checkout to receive a generous 10% discount on classes for additional siblings, enabling all your children to embark on a shared educational journey together.

It's our mission at Blue Ridge Boost to provide a supportive and enriching learning environment for your children. With these discount codes, we hope to make our classes more affordable and encourage your family to embrace the joy of learning together.

Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to ignite your child's curiosity and foster a love for lifelong learning. Visit our website or contact our friendly customer service team to explore our class offerings and take advantage of these exclusive discount codes today.
